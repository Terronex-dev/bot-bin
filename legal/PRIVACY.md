# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Overview

Bot-BIN is local-first software. Your data stays on your machine.

## What We Don't Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track usage
- We do not transmit your documents anywhere

## External Services

If you configure an external embedding provider (OpenAI, Voyage, etc.), your document content will be sent to that service for processing. Review their privacy policies:

- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Voyage AI Privacy Policy](https://www.voyageai.com/privacy)

## Local Processing

When using local embedding models (e.g., via Ollama), all processing happens on your machine. No data leaves your system.
